About - Maison Fleur Beauty Lounge

Maison Fleur Beauty Lounge was established on June 12, 2022. The name MAISON FLEUR is gotten from french words MASION meaning HOUSE and FLEUR meaning FLOWER thus meaning HOUSE OF FLOWER This brand provides you with services like:

  • Installation
  • Hair Revamp
  • Hair Coloring
  • Make-up Sessions
  • Bridal Stylings and more...

Our physical store is located at Lasu-Isheri Expressway, Ipaye Market, Peace Estate, Ojo, Lagos.

Our Founder Okpe Flora is a Certified Hairstylist and Make-up Artist. One of the best in Lagos State. This brand came to life all from her hardwork. Her aim and objective is to render Afforbale and Luxury Beauty Services to make you look CHIC!. We're always at your service.
